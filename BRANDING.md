# ForgeSyte Branding

ForgeSyte is positioned as a **vision core** you might find wired into a starship’s engineering deck. The tone is serious, industrial, and precise, with a hint of speculative technology.

---

## Naming

ForgeSyte = “Forge” (creation, shaping, engineering) + “Syte” (sight / site / system).

Represents a system where raw visual data is smelted into structured intelligence.

---
